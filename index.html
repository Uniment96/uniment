Uniment is built specifically for restaurant operators in the UAE and wider GCC region. All revenue figures are tracked in AED, and the platform is designed around the operational realities of Dubai's fast-paced F&B market — from multi-branch Jumeirah restaurants to high-volume delivery operations in Abu Dhabi and Sharjah.

FAQ

Short answers to common questions.

Is RevenueRoll free for UAE restaurants?

Yes — you can access the current RevenueRoll dashboard at no cost via the link above. Pricing evolves as integrations and advanced features expand.

Can I use Uniment for multiple branches across the UAE?

Yes — Uniment is built for multi-branch operations. Role-based access ensures each manager sees only their branch's data, whether you're running locations in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, or Sharjah.

Does Uniment work with UAE delivery platforms?

Uniment tracks channel mix including Talabat, Deliveroo, Noon Food, and direct orders. Import via POS export today — direct API integrations are on the roadmap.

Will Uniment support UAE VAT reporting?

UAE VAT-aware cost reporting is planned as part of the Uniment Inventory module. Revenue is already tracked in AED, and FTA-compliant food cost records are in development.

Is my restaurant's data private and secure?

Absolutely. Uniment uses multi-tenant architecture with full data isolation — no other operator can access your data. Privacy-first analytics, no selling of data.
